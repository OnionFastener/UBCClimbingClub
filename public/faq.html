How do I purchase a membership?

Show up to any event with $20. We only take CASH.

Do i have to be a ubc student to join

No, our club is open to UBC and non-UBC students alike.

What does my membership give me access to?

Your membership gives you access to instructional workshops, trips, socials, exclusive discounts, and access to an awesome community of climbers until the beginning of the following school year.
